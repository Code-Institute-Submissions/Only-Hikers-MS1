About Us

I'm Helen (on the left) and that's my sister Jane in the photo with me.

I got back from living in Canada in March (thanks to Covid!). Vancouver was definitely where my love for hiking really began. Most people there have a passion for the great outdoors and weekends usually involved some sort of activity in the mountains.

Since moving back to Ireland, I have been spending weekends exploring hiking trails on this beautiful island of ours.

My goal is to start organising hikes in small groups in different parts of the country each week/fortnight (TBC!). We are awaiting government guidelines before we proceed with our events page.

In the meantime, please feel free to contact us with any questions or comments or check out our gallery for a "peak" of what's to come.
