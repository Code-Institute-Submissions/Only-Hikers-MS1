About Us

I'm Helen and beside me in the picture is my sister Jane. I recently got back from living in Canada which is where my love for hiking began. Most people there have a passion for the great outdoors and it definitely rubbed off on me.

Since moving home, I have spent my time (when not in lockdown!) exploring our country and all the hiking trails it has to offer. Why is it we usually take what's on our doorstep for granted?

Our goal is to start organising hikes in the near future so we can hopefully inspire more people to get out and take a hike!

Please be patient as we try to set up our first hiking event but we are waiting for the government to release the new guidelines before we make any decisions on dates.

In the meantime, please feel free to contact us with any questions or comments.
Or check out our gallery for a "peak" of what's to come.
